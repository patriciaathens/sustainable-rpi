Reduce & Reuse First, Then Recycle:

Though recycling does play an important role in reducing your carbon footprint, reducing and reusing the materials you use plays a much bigger role. Remember that recycling still produces extra waste, since energy and resources are required to transport and break down materials. Additionally, most people do not sort their recycling properly, recycled materials have a significant decrease in quality, and only a certain percentage of recycled materials after processing are able to be used again.

First start by reducing the amount of waste you use by not buying into wasteful products. Think about the packaging, production, and reusability of the product. Then, reuse what you can.
